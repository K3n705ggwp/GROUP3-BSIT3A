OBJECTIVES

General Objective

This study aims to develop a Parcel Lockbox with Centralized Management System to provide a secure, automated, and efficient solution for unattended deliveries. It also seeks to address challenges related to missed deliveries and enhance last-mile delivery efficiency.

Specific Objectives

This study specifically aims to:

  1. Design and develop a smart parcel lockbox system that can: 
                 a. Detect lockbox location;
           b. Dispense payment automatically;
           c. Allow users to lease a lockbox;;
           d. Monitor lockbox status;
           e. Generate analytical reports on delivery performance of various shipping companies and online shopping platforms;
  2. Evaluate the system based on the Technology Acceptance Model (TAM), namely Perceived Usefulness (PU) and Perceived Ease of Use (PEOU).

General Objective:

The study aimed to develop a Parcel Lockbox with Centralized Management System for the purpose of providing a secure, automated, and efficient solution for unattended deliveries, while addressing the challenges of missed deliveries and enhancing last-mile deliveries.

Specific Objectives:

1. Design and develop a smart parcel lockbox system that can:

Parcel Verification

Capture and verify parcel deliveries using an ESP32 camera and object detection via Edge Impulse;

Security

Secure parcels using PIN-based access mechanism

Centralized Management

enable landlords, business managers, or school administrators to rent out compartments for secure deliveries via a centralized management system.

Automated Cash Disbursement

Facilitate Cash-on-Delivery (COD) transaction through an automated money dispenser
